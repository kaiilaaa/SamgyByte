About Us

SamgyByte is a unique Korean-inspired restaurant that specializes in serving high-quality samgyeopsal rice meals. Our mission is to introduce Samgy Rice as a flavorful and budget-friendly meal option, combining the rich taste of grilled Korean-style pork belly with well-cooked rice and a variety of distinctive sauces. We aim to create an immersive dining experience that showcases the richness of Korean culinary traditions.
